Privacy Policy

Your Privacy Matters - Comprehensive Guide to Data Protection

This Privacy Policy explains how electrosab collects, uses, discloses, and safeguards your information when you use our services, visit our website electrosab.site, or engage with us through other channels. By accessing our services, you agree to the terms outlined in this policy.

1. Information Collection Practices

a. Directly Provided Information

We collect information you voluntarily provide through:

  • Account registration (name, email, password)
  • Purchase transactions (billing address, payment details)
  • Customer support interactions
  • Newsletter subscriptions
  • Survey responses and contest entries

b. Automatically Collected Information

We automatically collect technical data through:

  • Cookies and tracking technologies
  • Server logs (IP address, browser type)
  • Device information (OS version, mobile identifiers)
  • Usage patterns (pages visited, clickstream data)

c. Third-Party Sources

We may receive information from:

  • Social media platforms (when you connect accounts)
  • Payment processors (transaction confirmations)
  • Marketing partners (campaign performance data)
  • Public databases and identity verification services

2. Use of Collected Information

We process your information to:

  • Process and fulfill orders
  • Provide personalized recommendations
  • Improve website functionality and user experience
  • Conduct market research and analysis
  • Prevent fraudulent activities
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Send promotional communications (with consent)

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

a. Service Providers

We share data with essential service providers:

  • Payment processors (Stripe, PayPal)
  • Shipping carriers (UPS, FedEx)
  • Cloud storage providers (AWS, Google Cloud)
  • Marketing platforms (Mailchimp, HubSpot)

b. Legal Requirements

We may disclose information when required by:

  • Court orders or legal processes
  • Government investigations
  • Regulatory compliance requests

c. Business Transfers

Information may be transferred during mergers, acquisitions, or asset sales.

4. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries outside your residence. We ensure adequate protection through:

  • EU Standard Contractual Clauses
  • Privacy Shield certification (where applicable)
  • Binding Corporate Rules

5. Data Security Measures

We implement robust security protocols including:

  • SSL/TLS encryption for data transmission
  • Regular security audits
  • Role-based access controls
  • Anonymization/pseudonymization techniques
  • PCI DSS compliance for payment data

6. User Rights and Choices

a. Access & Correction

You may request:

  • Access to personal data we hold
  • Correction of inaccurate information
  • Data portability in machine-readable format

b. Deletion Rights

You may request deletion of personal data, subject to legal retention requirements.

c. Marketing Preferences

Manage communication preferences through:

  • Account settings dashboard
  • Email unsubscribe links
  • Cookie consent manager

7. Retention Periods

We retain data based on:

  • Active account status (while account remains active)
  • Legal requirements (tax records for 7 years)
  • Business needs (transaction history for warranty purposes)

8. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed at individuals under 16. We do not knowingly collect children's data without parental consent.

9. Policy Updates

We may periodically update this policy. Material changes will be communicated through:

  • Website banners
  • Email notifications
  • Account dashboard alerts
